Power to break the rules.

OTHERWORLD STONE is a material with id #40. Its rarity value is 5(Proficiency-Made).It involves further crafting recipes but cannot be directly crafted.

It involves as a material of NATURE CORE , BLOOD SUBSTANCE , LEGEND SOUL .

OTHERWORLD STONE can only be dropped after the OTHERWORLD INVASION after the STORM is defeated the first time.

OTHERWORLD STONE will be removed after ABYSS EYE is defeated, so its results should be crafted earlier.
