Underia
Endless evil.

EVIL INGOT is a material with id #45. Its rarity value is 5(Proficiency-Made).It involves further crafting recipes but cannot be directly crafted.It can be dropped by entities.

It involves as a material of BALANCED STABBER , DISCORD STORM , SPIRITUAL KNIFE , EVIL BOOK , BLOOD SACRIFICE , KARMIC FIRE , MURAMASA , FORBIDDEN OATH , FORBIDDEN CURSE: DEATH , THE FINAL INGOT , EVIL PICKAXE . It can be dropped by Cells, Evil Mark, Evil Ore, Ice Cap, Lazer Fish, Life Watcher, Light Fly, Mechanic Eye, Polar Cube, Polar Snowman, Pressure Eye, Red Corruption, Red Corruption, Snow Drake, Soul Flower, and 2 more.
