SOUL OF JUSTICE is a material with id #68. Its rarity value is 6(Epic).It involves further crafting recipes but cannot be directly crafted.It can be dropped by entities.

It involves as a material of EMPTY GUN , JUSTICE SHOTGUN , CRITICALLY , FROZEN FIRE EXPLODER , BOW OF SANCTION , INCREMENTAL SPIRIT ESSENCE , LAZER RAIN , LAZER GUN , LAZER SNIPER , COWBOY HAT , SOUL OF DETERMINATION , ROCKET LAUNCHER V3.0 , SOUL OF DETERMINATION . It can be dropped by Devil Python.
