ASCENDANT SPIRIT ESSENCE is a material with id #91. Its rarity value is 11(Super Mythic).It is craftable and involves further crafting recipes.

It involves as a material of MY RING , ETERNAL ECHO , STAR OF DEVOTION , RELEVATION OF CYCLES , MIND , ADHERENT , WORLD'S LORD , ULTRA-LIGHTSPEED , BEYOND HORIZON , GRASP OF THE INFINITE CORRIDOR , KARMIC TRAIL BOOTS , FATE ALIGNMENT AMULET , ZENITH , HEART LOCKET , YELLOW FLOWER .

ASCENDANT SPIRIT ESSENCE is the final upgrade of the type SPIRIT ESSENCE in SPIRIT ESSENCE , INCREMENTAL SPIRIT ESSENCE and ORIGIN SPIRIT ESSENCE . Like other items of this item, it is used to combine weapons to a final-leveled strong weapon, including ZENITH .
