THE FINAL INGOT is a material with id #81. Its rarity value is 11(Super Mythic).It is craftable and involves further crafting recipes.It can also be dropped by entities.

It involves as a material of My Ring, Galaxy Broadsword, Highlight, Turning Point, Resolution, Climax, Falling Action, Rising Action, Eternal Echo, Star of Devotion, Relevation of Cycles, Ultra-Lightspeed, Beyond Horizon, Grasp of the Infinite Corridor, Karmic Trail Boots, and 7 more.It can be dropped by Omega Flowey.

THE FINAL INGOT is made by all other "ingots" and several "final" workstations. The ORE of this ingot is REASON and RESULT.

It contains the power of KARMA to make karmic weapons and armor.
