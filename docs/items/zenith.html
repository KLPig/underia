ZENITH is a melee weapon with id #209. Its rarity value is 12(Godlike).It can be directly crafted.

ZENITH is also a weapon. It deals 0 physical damage, 0 thinking damage. It acts as a complex weapon. It attacks every 13 ticks, i.e 6.15 per second. It's "dashboard" dpm is 0.0.

Recipe Tree

ZENITH is the final melee weapons of UNDERIA. Even its basic damage value depends on the melee damage addons (basic 15 physical damage and 45 thinking damage.

ZENITH damages rapidly. When attacking, it glows light and throw all the weapons in its recipe tree(old) in a quite fast frequency. Note that these melee weapons does NOT trigger immune ticks in ALL difficulty modes so each damage will be dealt.

Press q to throw all the sub-weapons at ONCE. The CD is about 1s.

ZENITH is directly inspired from ZENITH in Terraria, and it's attack mode seems to be similar.
