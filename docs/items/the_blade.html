THE BLADE is a melee weapon with id #190. Its rarity value is 8(Masterwork).It is craftable and involves further crafting recipes.

It involves as a material of WORLD'S LORD , ZENITH .

THE BLADE is also a weapon. It deals 300 physical damage. It acts as a complex weapon. It attacks every 45 ticks, i.e 1.78 per second. It's "dashboard" dpm is 37312.0.

THE BLADE is a direct material of ZENITH , the final melee weapon and however can also be crafted into WORLD'S LORD

THE BLADE attacks 16 times at a cycle. At first 14 cycles, it shoots 1 and 2 strong beams twice and then throw out and shoots several small beams.
